Fairness in Every Ride, One Fare at a Time

A Fare Metric Advocacy

What Is It and How It Works

  • Base Fare: A fixed starting fee.
  • Distance and Time: Fare increases based on the distance, time of day, and duration.
  • Fuel Adjustment: Real-time updates reflecting gas prices to ensure fairness for both riders and drivers.

Our Vision

This system eliminates unfair fares, ensures pricing transparency, and makes operational costs predictable for providers.

  • Eliminate Unfair Fares: This system eliminates unfair fares, making pricing transparent.
  • Predictable Fares: Passengers benefit from predictable fares, while drivers avoid revenue loss due to fluctuating costs like gas prices.
  • Covers Operational Costs: Ensures that transport providers can cover their operational costs without hidden fees.

How to Achieve This Vision

  • Partner with Local Governments: Collaborate on pilot programs and create policies to mandate fare transparency.
  • Work with Transport Companies: Engage ride-hailing apps, taxi, and jeepney operators to implement the system in their services.
